A-YOS MVP Plan
Product Overview
A-YOS is a service marketplace that connects customers with local workers for on-demand or scheduled services. The MVP focuses on verified accounts, simple booking, nearby-worker matching, cash-payment tracking, and post-service feedback.
Core MVP Features
1. Customer Registration and Verification
Customers can create an account and submit a valid government-issued ID for verification. Verified accounts help build trust and reduce fraudulent activity.
2. Worker Registration and Profile Setup
Service providers can register as workers, submit a valid ID, select their service categories, define their service area, and add relevant work experience. Admin approval is required before a worker can receive bookings.
3. Service Request Creation
Customers can create a service request by providing:
Service category
Description of the problem or task
Preferred date and time
Exact location
Optional photo upload
Photos help workers understand the required service before accepting a booking.
4. Smart Worker Matching
The platform recommends suitable workers based on:
Service category and skills
Customer and worker location
Service area
Availability
Worker rating
Distance from the customer
For the MVP, this can begin as rule-based matching and be enhanced with AI recommendations later.
5. Booking Management
Customers can request, schedule, monitor, and manage bookings. Workers can view incoming requests and accept or decline them.
Booking statuses include:
Pending
Accepted
In Progress
Completed
Cancelled
6. Location and Point-to-Point Services
A-YOS uses location data to show nearby workers and match bookings within a worker’s service area. The system records the customer’s service location and the worker’s starting location to estimate travel distance and support directions.
7. Cash Payment Confirmation
The MVP supports cash payments only. After a completed service, the customer and worker can confirm that payment was received. The platform securely records the transaction details, including booking reference, amount, date, and payment status.
8. Ratings and Reviews
After a booking is completed, customers can rate the worker and leave a review. Ratings and feedback are displayed on worker profiles to help future customers make informed choices.
9. Filipino–English Translation
The app translates messages between Filipino and English to help customers and workers communicate clearly. Translation is limited to these two languages for the MVP.
10. Admin Dashboard
Administrators can manage and monitor the platform through a dashboard with tools to:
Verify or reject customer and worker IDs
Manage user and worker accounts
Review worker categories and profiles
Monitor bookings and cash-payment records
Moderate ratings and reviews
Handle reports, disputes, and suspicious activity
View basic platform activity and statistics

A-YOS is a local service marketplace that connects homeowners with verified workers within the same subdivision. The MVP focuses on trusted registration, nearby-worker matching, booking management, cash-payment tracking, commissions, and service feedback.
The platform does not include public “post a job to find a worker” listings. Instead, customers submit a private service request and receive matching worker recommendations.
Core MVP Features
1. Customer Registration and Verification
Homeowners can create accounts and submit a valid government-issued ID for verification. Verified accounts help maintain a safe and trusted community.
2. Worker Registration and Profile Setup
Workers can register, submit a valid ID, choose their service categories, define their availability, and list relevant experience. Workers require admin approval before receiving bookings.
3. Private Service Requests
Homeowners can request a service by providing:
Service category
Description of the problem or task
Preferred date and time
Exact location within the subdivision
Optional photo upload
Service requests are not published as public posts. They are used only to match the homeowner with suitable workers.
4. Subdivision-Based Service Area
The initial service area is limited to a single subdivision. The system uses location details to recommend workers who are nearby and available within that subdivision.
Future versions may expand service coverage to nearby communities.
5. Smart Worker Matching and Recommendations
A-YOS recommends workers based on:
Service category and worker skills
Location within the subdivision
Worker availability
Customer ratings and reviews
Distance from the homeowner
Worker recommendation subscription status
Workers with an active recommendation subscription may receive priority placement in relevant recommendations, while still considering suitability, availability, and ratings.
6. Worker Recommendation Subscription
Workers may subscribe to be prioritized in customer recommendations. This subscription helps improve their visibility when customers search for relevant services.
Subscription pricing, duration, and activation are managed by the admin.
7. Booking Management
Homeowners can request, schedule, track, and manage bookings. Workers can review private requests and accept or decline them.
Booking statuses include:
Pending
Accepted
In Progress
Completed
Cancelled
8. Point-to-Point Location Details
The system records the homeowner’s service location and the worker’s starting location to support matching, distance estimation, and possible directions within the subdivision.
9. Cash Payment and Commission Tracking
The MVP supports cash payments only.
Once a service is completed and cash payment is confirmed, the system records the payment and calculates the platform commission. The initial worker commission is 10% of the completed booking amount.
Example: For a ₱1,000 completed service, the worker receives ₱900 and ₱100 is recorded as the A-YOS commission.
The platform securely stores the booking reference, service amount, commission amount, payment date, and confirmation status. 
10. Homeowner Commission via HOA Collaboration 
A-YOS supports a separate service charge for homeowners. For the initial MVP, the homeowner charge is set to ₱0.
This charge remains configurable in the admin dashboard for future use.
11. Ratings and Reviews
After a completed booking, homeowners can rate workers and leave feedback. Ratings and reviews appear on worker profiles to help other homeowners select trusted providers.
12. Filipino–English Translation
The app translates messages between Filipino and English to help homeowners and workers communicate clearly. The MVP supports only these two languages.
13. Admin Dashboard
Administrators can manage and monitor the platform through a dashboard with tools to:
Verify or reject homeowner and worker IDs
Manage accounts, worker profiles, and service categories
Monitor bookings and cash-payment confirmations
Set and update the worker commission rate, initially 10%
Set and update the homeowner service charge, initially ₱0
Manage worker recommendation subscriptions
Moderate ratings, reviews, reports, and disputes
View basic activity, booking, commission, and subscription records
